WATER ISSUES

Climate Changes

About

It is a software developed for NASA Hackathon, as part of the challenge proposed in 2019.

This solution is a mobile app that communicates users (local population) about the impacts of rising oceans in the world (and in their own lives), showing them these changes graphically. The program is capable of realizing simulations, as well as alerting people if a disaster is on the way. All of this is only possible because data is being received in real-time from monitoring platforms (NASA Databases and others).

The platform can be used by anyone, as it is an idea with global applicability. However, it was developed with a focus on those who lives in critical areas, near coastal zones, as they are at risk.

There is an exclusive channel for the population to share information about news (locally and globally) and to be notified automatically by the system about risk events, in order to prevent and evacuate the delicate places.

The next step is investing in a development team, for the purpose of creating an Augmented Reality Platform to change the way simulations are visualized, so that it is more interactive and engages the user. The company will also develop a chat system, so people can talk to each other.
